# Scope & Ethics

## Purpose
Competitive / market research on a professional in a stated role, using public
professional context. Typical lawful uses: understanding a competitor's product
leadership, market positioning, and public strategy signals.

## In scope
- Public professional profiles, company pages, press/newsroom, published talks,
  papers/patents, public professional social posts, and the seed URLs you provide.

## Out of scope (refused by the code)
- Home/personal address, personal contact details, family, health, finances, religion,
  political affiliation, ethnicity, or any special-category data.
- Data-broker / people-search sources.
- **Psychographic profiling and inner-motivation inference** — the harness reports what a
  person has publicly said/done professionally, not a psychological model of them.
- Surveillance, tracking, or any non-public collection.

## Your responsibilities
You must have a lawful basis (GDPR Art. 6 legitimate interest, or equivalent), respect
platform terms and robots directives, avoid automated decisions about the individual,
and honor objection/erasure requests. This tool does not make those judgments for you.
